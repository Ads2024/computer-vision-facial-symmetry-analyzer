About Facial Symmetry Analysis

Facial symmetry analysis is a powerful tool for understanding and quantifying facial features and their alignment. Our analyzer uses advanced computer vision techniques to provide detailed insights into facial symmetry.

How It Works

Our analyzer uses MediaPipe Face Mesh to detect facial landmarks and calculate symmetry metrics across different facial zones. The analysis includes:

  • Facial landmark detection
  • Symmetry score calculation
  • Zone-specific analysis
  • Visual comparison tools

Understanding Scores

The symmetry score is calculated based on the alignment of corresponding facial features. A lower score indicates better symmetry. The analysis considers:

  • Overall facial symmetry
  • Individual zone scores
  • Perceptual importance weights
  • Detailed asymmetry mapping
